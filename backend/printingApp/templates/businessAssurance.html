Tax Implications:
There are significant tax benefits afforded to RA investors. You are permitted to deduct up to 27.5% of your annual taxable incomes (subject to R350 000 per year maximum). Contributions in excess of this amount may deducted in the following tax year or at retirement. In addition to this, no income tax or capital gain tax is charged on the investment returns within an RA.
